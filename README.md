**Coke-Sales-Analysis**
Project Overview:-

This project provides a comprehensive analysis of Coca-Cola beverage sales across various U.S. regions. It includes raw sales data, data cleaning steps, KPI calculations, pivot-based insights, and final conclusions aimed at supporting strategic business decision-making.

Project Structure:-

The Excel workbook contains the following sheets:

Data (Raw Sales Dataset):-

Includes transactional fields such as:

Retailer name

Invoice date, region, state, city

Beverage brand

Price per unit, units sold, total sales

Operating profit and operating margin

Beginner (Basic Cleaning Tasks):-

Instructions and exercises for:

Data formatting

Removing duplicates

Standardizing text and categories

Correcting data types

Basic summary calculations

Intermediate (KPI & Pivot Analysis):-

This sheet covers analytical tasks including:

Total sales by retailer

Sales by state and region

Units sold distribution

Operating margin comparison

Pivot tables for different performance metrics

Advanced (Deep Insights):-

Includes advanced Excel workflows such as:

Profitability analysis

Trend identification

Retailer efficiency evaluation

Price sensitivity analysis

Multi-level pivot dashboards

Key Insights:-

Retailers show significant variation in total sales and units sold.

Some states/regions contribute a majority share of overall revenue.

High unit sales do not always correspond to high profit due to margin differences.

A few top retailers account for a large percentage of total Coke sales.

Certain mid-tier markets show strong potential for future growth.

Conclusion:-

This project highlights major sales patterns across U.S. retailers and provides actionable insights for strategic planning. The structured approach—from raw data to advanced pivots—helps understand:

Which retailers perform best

Which markets need improvement

How margins impact profitability

Where Coca-Cola can focus to enhance distribution and revenue
